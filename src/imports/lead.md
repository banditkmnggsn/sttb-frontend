# Role
Expert UX/UI Designer for Educational Landing Pages and Bootcamps.

# Task
Redesign the "LEAD Center" (Learning, Equipping, & Development) page for STT Bandung. Transform a text-heavy academic page into an engaging, modern landing page for a non-formal training and certification center.

# Visual Style
- **Theme:** Inspiring, Dynamic, and Professional.
- **Color Palette:** Deep Navy Blue (#002147) for grounding, bright Gold for action buttons, and a secondary soft Teal or Light Blue to represent "Growth/Learning".
- **Typography:** Playfair Display for authoritative section headers. Plus Jakarta Sans or Inter for clean, legible body text.
- **Aesthetic:** Clean "SaaS-style" landing page features. Use large icons, ample whitespace, overlapping image cards, and soft drop-shadows.

# Page Structure & Components

1. **Hero Section (The Hook):**
   - Background: High-quality image of professionals or students in a dynamic workshop/seminar setting, with a dark Navy overlay.
   - Headline (Large & Bold): "L.E.A.D. Center: Memperlengkapi Pelayan, Membangun Tubuh Kristus."
   - Sub-headline: "Pusat Pendidikan & Pelatihan Non-Formal STT Bandung (Learning, Equipping, & Development)."
   - Call to Action (CTA) Buttons: Primary Gold button ["Lihat Program & Kelas"], Secondary outlined white button ["Tentang LEAD"].

2. **The 3 Pillars (Bento Grid or 3-Column Cards):**
   - Replace the heavy text explanation with three distinct, beautifully illustrated cards:
     - **Card 1: Learning.** Icon: Book/Brain. Title: "Kelas Audit & Kuliah Umum". Text: "Ikuti kelas teologi formal secara sit-in sesuai kebutuhan Anda."
     - **Card 2: Equipping.** Icon: Shield/Tools. Title: "Sertifikasi & Pelatihan". Text: "Program khusus seperti Perspectives Study Program dan Little STEP."
     - **Card 3: Development.** Icon: Chart/Target. Title: "Pengembangan Modul". Text: "Pembuatan bahan ajar dan kurikulum untuk pelayanan gerejawi dan dunia kerja."
   - Hover state: Cards should elevate slightly with a Gold bottom border.

3. **Featured Programs (Carousel/Horizontal Scroll):**
   - Section Title: "Program Unggulan Kami"
   - Create sleek event cards for active programs mentioned in the data (e.g., "Vocatio Marketplace Fellow", "Perspectives", "Little STEP").
   - Cards should have: Program Logo/Image, Title, Short Description, Date/Batch info, and a "Daftar Sekarang" (Register Now) CTA.

4. **Event Calendar (Modern UI Upgrade):**
   - Redesign the current clunky calendar. 
   - Use a clean "Upcoming Events" list view. Left side: Big Date Square (e.g., "23 FEB"). Right side: Event title ("Little STEP #5"), Time, Location (Zoom/On-site), and a quick "Join" link.

5. **Media & Resources (Video Highlight):**
   - Section Title: "Galeri & Media"
   - A large embedded video player UI (e.g., for "City TransForMission") with a play button overlay, placed next to a short description of the video series.

# Constraints
- Break up long paragraphs at all costs. Use bullet points, bold text, and icons.
- The design must look like a modern "Upskilling/Bootcamp" platform to appeal to working professionals and marketplace ministries.
- Keep the layout highly responsive, prioritizing a stacked layout for mobile.
